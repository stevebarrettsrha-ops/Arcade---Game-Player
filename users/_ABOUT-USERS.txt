Each player profile lives here in its own folder, created automatically
when someone makes a profile on the ARCADE screen (name + 4-digit PIN):

   users/<name>/profile.json     (name + PIN, PIN stored hashed)
   users/<name>/saves/<system>/<game>.state   (their save states)
   users/<name>/saves/<system>/<game>.srm      (their in-game battery saves)

You don't edit these by hand. To remove a player, delete their folder.
Note: the 4-digit PIN keeps players' saves separate on your home network;
it is light protection, not strong security. Back up this folder to keep
saves safe. Profiles/saves are NOT included in the download.
