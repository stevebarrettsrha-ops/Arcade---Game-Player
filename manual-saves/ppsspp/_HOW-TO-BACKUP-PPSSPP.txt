PSP (PPSSPP) SAVES — backup folder
==================================

PPSSPP keeps its PSP "memory stick" - your game saves AND save states -
in your web browser's storage. They persist between sessions on the SAME
browser and are not lost in normal use.

BEST WAY TO KEEP THEM SAFE:
  - Open PPSSPP once while online, then use your browser's "Install app"
    / "Add to Home Screen" option. Once installed it keeps its data
    reliably and also runs offline.
  - Keep using the same browser for PPSSPP, and do NOT clear that site's
    data, or the PSP saves will be lost.

PPSSPP is a separate app with its own save system, so its saves are NOT
stored in the per-player users/ folders the cartridge systems use, and
ARCADE cannot move them automatically.

TO KEEP A MANUAL COPY HERE:
  - If PPSSPP's own menus offer a memory-stick / save-data export, save
    the exported file into this folder. Otherwise your saves remain in
    the browser as described above.
  - If several people share this machine, make a subfolder per person
    in here (e.g. manual-saves/ppsspp/zoe/).
